Ready to sail with Us?

We’re excited to welcome new members to our sailing community! Whether you’re an experienced sailor or just starting to explore the beauty of life on the water, our club offers a place for everyone. By joining, you’ll become part of a passionate and supportive group that shares your love for the water

To get started, click the button below and you’ll be redirected to our Microsoft Forms to fill in your details. A member of our team will review your submission and get back to you as soon as possible with all the details you need to begin your journey with us.

Joining takes just a few minutes, but it’s your first step towards an exciting adventure filled with learning, new friendships, and unforgettable sailing experiences.
